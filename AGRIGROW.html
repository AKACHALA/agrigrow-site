Quick Farming Tips

Small improvements can make a big difference in productivity. These are strong starting points for any farm.

Prepare the Soil

Test soil condition, use manure or compost, and avoid over-cultivation.

Monitor Plant Health

Inspect leaves and stems often to catch pests, disease, and nutrient problems early.

Protect Livestock

Provide clean water, dry shelter, balanced feed, and timely vaccination.
